📋How to Manage These Sections in Admin

📚 Academy Sections Tab

  1. Click "Academy Sections" in the admin sidebar
  2. View the 6 core learning features currently shown
  3. Edit title, description, emoji icon, and order
  4. Activate/deactivate features anytime
  5. These will flip-reveal on hover for visitors

💬 Academy Community Tab

  1. Click "Academy Community" in the admin sidebar
  2. View the 6 recent community posts shown above
  3. Add new posts with type, content, and author name
  4. Posts auto-create with today's date
  5. Visitors see your most active community on the homepage
